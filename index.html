ABOUT US

SignSparks is a student-led organization that aims to give hope and inspiration to students out there with a hearing impairment. We believe that nothing should stand in the way of someone's dreams. Our team here at SignSparks tries their very best to offer students who are hearing-impaired a chance to explore and learn the difference languages of computer science.































Our Computer Science Courses

The courses we offer here include JavaScript and Python, with Java and HTML curriculum courses on in future plans. It doesn't matter if you're an extreme beginner with coding or a complete pro; we're here to help guide you and nurture your interests to make them stronger. Starting from simple introductory lessons, our courses soon dive deep into the complex roots of each programming language, and you'll see why they're so important in our everyday lives.-































Why SignSparks is so Special

As mentioned previously, the SignSparks team offers something that not many other tutoring organizations do: specific CS courses dedicated to and shaped around students with hearing loss. We believe that such a trait should not make a difference in how the student should learn or pursue their dreams. We nurture children to be kind, intelligent, and most importantly, confident in their abilities, whether they have a hearing impairment or not.
