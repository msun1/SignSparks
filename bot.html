MEET SAMMY

Meet Sammy: our very own SignSparks discord bot! She can help with any CS, logistical, and ASL questions. What's more is that she has an integrated camera system that identifies sign language and turns it into real-time words for our special hearing impaired students.

We believe what makes SignSparks stands out is the fact that we offer interactive ASL live lessions as well as Sammy, who loves to help students answer any questions.
